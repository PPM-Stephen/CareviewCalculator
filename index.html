Careview Rate - Time Calculator

This is for usage in cases where invoice document values cannot be easily calculated within careview (e.g. time = 0.33hours). To obtain values for rate and time that will satisfy the invoice requirements, simply input your desired amount for a service in careview (that will lead to the desired invoice total) and supply the maximum possible rate for that service. You may then pick from a list of values produced in the results zone.
